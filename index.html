Why get Linux instead of Windows or MacOS?

Linux is a group of open-source operating systems that offer flexibility, performance, and privacy. Many people choose Linux for the following reasons:

  • Runs well on older or low-end hardware
  • Fewer background processes and less resource overhead for many distributions.
  • Strong community and long-term support on many distributions (LTS releases).
  • Open-source: transparent development and large ecosystem of free software.
  • Excellent for developers, and if you want to learn how operating systems work.

Who should try Linux?

Linux is amazing for:

  • Developers who need a reliable, script-friendly environment.
  • Users wanting privacy or control.
  • People with older or lower end hardware who need a lightweight operating system.
